About me

My name is Jethro Jorgino Dipotaroeno and I graduated a Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) degree in Multimedia Technology in 2007. I have been crafting creatively since I was a kid and when I grew up I knew I had to do something with it in this digital environment.

Behind the site...

This page is a responsive single page website which can be approached by different devices in their viewport. I wrote the CSS with Sass and used some elements of the Compass library. The animations are made using scrollMagic & GSAP, Green Socket Animation Platform and glued together with jQuery.
